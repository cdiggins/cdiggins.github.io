About the Blog

My name is Christopher Diggins and I have been writing software for over 20 years. I currently work as a Principal Software Developer at Autodesk. I am very interested in the craft of software development, and in particular the role that programming languages play in enabling us to produce better quality software more quickly.

My current open-source project is Myna an open-source text parsing library for JavaScript.

About the Blog

My blog, like many of my older blogs, will cover various topics related to the craft of software development and in particular the design and implementation of programming languages.
